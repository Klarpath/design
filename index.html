Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Here are some of the most common things we get asked.

Why is there a monthly fee if I own the code?

For the Custom Scale plan, the setup fee covers the bespoke design and development work. The Growth Starter plan has no setup fee.

The monthly fee covers your premium hosting, domain name, and professional email. It also includes daily backups, security, and content updates. For Custom Scale clients, the monthly fee also covers advanced AI-driven SEO and dedicated account management.

Are there any usage restrictions on the code?

Yes. You receive full ownership of the final website code for use in your specific business or project. This gives you the freedom to host it anywhere and make any modifications you need.

However, the license you receive is for your usage alone. The underlying design, templates, and framework remain our intellectual property. Therefore, the website cannot be resold, redistributed, or repackaged as a template for other clients. This policy allows us to continue offering premium, unique designs at an affordable price.

What happens if I cancel my monthly plan?

Because you own the code, the website is yours to keep. If you cancel, we will package up all your website files and send them to you. You would then be responsible for finding a new hosting provider and handling all future maintenance and security yourself. You are never locked in.

How long does it take to get my website live?

The timeline depends on the package and how quickly you provide your content. For Growth Starter, we typically launch in 1-2 weeks. For Custom Scale, which involves bespoke design and development, timelines are typically 4-8 weeks.

Can I update the website content myself?

Your monthly plan includes a set number of content updates per year, where we handle the changes for you. For users who are comfortable with code, you will have access to the source files (HTML/CSS) to make your own changes. However, most clients find it much easier and faster to let us handle it for them.

Why choose this over a builder like Squarespace or Wix?

Three main reasons: Performance, Ownership, and Professional Service. Our sites are hand-coded and optimized for speed, which is crucial for SEO and user experience. You get to own the code and can move it anywhere. Lastly, you get a dedicated developer to build and maintain your site, not just a drag-and-drop tool with limitations.

What do I need to provide to get started?

After you've chosen a package, we'll need your branding assets (logo, brand colors) and all the content for the website (text and images). We'll create a shared folder and guide you on the best formats to ensure everything looks perfect.
